About Me

My name is Sarah Peled. I am a Danish 22-year-old, currently studying Multimedia Design, at University College Lillebælt in Odense, Denmark. Studying Multimedia Design conists of going in depth with various fields, which means I have knowledgede and skills in different fields of work. I'm most passionate about web-development and web-design, which is why this field is my primary focus. Since I find it extremely annoying and frustrating when websites are confusing and difficult to use, I want to be able to help create websites that are user friendly. I like how this requires both being technical and being creative, as I have always been both.

I am definitely a logical and critical thinker, and I love learning new things. My favourite subjects in school were always math and science, and was never really fond of socialstudies or sports. Being creative is another quality of mine, and I enjoy being creative in many ways that not only include school and work. Since i can remember, I have always loved to draw and to paint, and this is where I find myself the most relaxed. I also enjoy baking, where I mostly love the decorating process, which is something I spend a lot of my free time doing.

My Life So Far

I was born November 4th 2000 in Odense, the third biggest city in Denmark, and have been living here since I was born. I have never been farther than 15 minutes away from friends or school, which has been super convinient. In 8th grade, there were two days where we had to visit a university and try out an education we thought might sound interesting. This is where I heard about HTML for the very first time. Here I was also introduced to the magic of Adobe Photoshop. I found these topics to be fascinating, and definitely felt an interest spark.

After mainly focusing on biotechnology and math during my higher education, I slowly drifted back into my fascination with web-development and web-design. This is why I this year started studying Multimediadesign.
